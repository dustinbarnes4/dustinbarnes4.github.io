Personal Interests

My days are spent working, studying, and being a dad. When I do get time to myself, I love participating in a variety of hobbies. I love woodworking and have several pieces of furniture that I've crafted myself. If I had limitless money, I would buy a large piece of land with a nice house on it, and build a giant woodshop and fill it with all sorts of tools.

I am also a fan of the Utah Jazz, and love watching every game I get a chance to see. Other interests of mine are reading, writing, watching movies, playing board games and video games, and listening to good music. With so many varied interests sometimes I find it hard to choose one to spend my time with.

Since my son was born in September of 2020, my favorite way to spend my time has been with him. He has recently begun recognizing me, and once he sees me for the firs time each day, he smiles really big and starts talking excitedly with me. There is nothing better in my life than being a father.
